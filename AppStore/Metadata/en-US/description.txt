CatLocal is a private field journal for the cats you meet.

Photograph a cat or choose a photo from your library. CatLocal uses Apple Vision on your iPhone to find the cat, separate it from the background, and turn the encounter into a tactile collectible card.

MAKE EACH ENCOUNTER YOURS

• Choose a card design
• Add an optional name and note
• Type a Memory Place to organize your private Catlas
• Revisit and edit saved cards anytime

PRIVATE BY DESIGN

• Cat detection and cutout creation happen on-device
• No account, analytics, advertising, or cloud database
• Cat photos and card details stay in local app storage
• CatLocal does not request GPS or save coordinates
• Stored originals are re-encoded without source EXIF or GPS metadata

Your collection belongs to you, on your iPhone.
